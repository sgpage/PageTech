Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Getting Started

What devices are compatible with DetectPro? +
DetectPro is compatible with iOS devices running iOS 14.0 or later, and Android devices running Android 8.0 or later. The app is optimized for both phones and tablets.
Is DetectPro free to use? +
DetectPro offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features including advanced statistics, unlimited cloud backup, and export capabilities are available through our subscription plans.
Does DetectPro work offline? +
Yes! DetectPro is designed to work offline. All your finds are stored locally on your device and will sync to the cloud when you next have an internet connection.

Using DetectPro

How accurate is the location tracking? +
DetectPro uses your device's GPS to record location data. Accuracy typically ranges from 3-10 meters depending on your device and environmental conditions. For best results, ensure you have a clear view of the sky when recording finds.
Can I edit a find after I've saved it? +
Absolutely! You can edit any find at any time by tapping on it in your database and selecting the edit option. All changes are automatically saved.
How many photos can I attach to a find? +
Free users can attach up to 3 photos per find. Premium subscribers can attach up to 10 photos per find.
Can I share my finds with other detectorists? +
Yes! You can share individual finds or entire collections via email, social media, or by exporting to PDF. You have full control over what information is shared.

Data and Privacy

Is my data secure? +
Your data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols and never share your personal information with third parties.
How do I backup my data? +
Free users can manually export their data. Premium subscribers benefit from automatic cloud backup, ensuring your finds are always safe. You can also enable local backups to your device.
Can I transfer my data to a new device? +
Yes! Sign in with your account on your new device and your data will automatically sync. Alternatively, you can export your data from one device and import it on another.

Technical Issues

The app crashes when I try to take a photo. What should I do? +
First, ensure DetectPro has camera permissions enabled in your device settings. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or your device. If you continue to experience issues, please report the fault using our fault reporting form.
My finds aren't syncing to the cloud. How can I fix this? +
Check that you have an active internet connection and that you're signed in to your account. Go to Settings > Sync and tap "Sync Now" to manually trigger a sync. If issues persist, try signing out and back in again.
I can't find an answer to my question here. What should I do? +
Please visit our support page or contact us directly at steve@pagetech.com. We're always happy to help!
