DetectPro User Manual

Complete guide to using DetectPro

Getting Started

Installation

DetectPro is available for iOS and Android devices. Download the app from your respective app store.

  1. Search for "DetectPro" in the App Store or Google Play
  2. Download and install the application
  3. Open the app and complete the initial setup
  4. Grant necessary permissions for location and camera access

First Time Setup

When you first launch DetectPro, you'll be guided through a quick setup process:

  • Create your detectorist profile
  • Set your preferred units of measurement
  • Configure location and notification settings

Recording Finds

Adding a New Find

  1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
  2. Take or select photos of your find
  3. Enter details such as type, material, and estimated period
  4. Your location will be automatically recorded
  5. Add any additional notes
  6. Save your find to the database

Viewing Your Finds

Access all your recorded finds from the main database view. You can:

  • Filter by date, type, or location
  • Search for specific finds
  • View finds on a map
  • Export your data

Advanced Features

Map View

The map view displays all your finds geographically. Tap any marker to view details of that find.

Statistics

View comprehensive statistics about your detecting activities, including:

  • Total number of finds
  • Finds by period
  • Finds by material
  • Most productive locations

Exporting Data

Export your finds database in various formats (CSV, PDF) for backup or sharing purposes.

Settings

Customize DetectPro to your preferences:

  • Display units (metric/imperial)
  • Date format
  • Privacy settings
  • Data backup options
  • Notification preferences

Tips and Best Practices

  • Always record finds immediately while details are fresh
  • Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
  • Be accurate with location data for future reference
  • Regularly backup your data
  • Keep the app updated for the latest features and improvements
